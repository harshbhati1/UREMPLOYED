Difference between Resume, CV(Curriculum Vitae), and Cover Letter -



Factors Resume CV Cover Letter
Length One page, sometimes two pages. Two pages or more. Less than one page.
Content Name, contact information, education, work experience, and related professional skills. Focus on work experiences. Name, contact information, education, work experience, and related professional skills. Includes a summary of your academic background, as well as your teaching and research experience, publications, presentations, awards, honors, affiliations, and other details. Brief information about the person, career experience, desired job profile, career goals.
Purpose Job applications In Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, employers expect a CV. In the United States, CVs are mainly used to apply for academic, educational, scientific, or research positions. To complete your CV or resume, briefly introduce yourself and explain your interests and suitability for the position.
