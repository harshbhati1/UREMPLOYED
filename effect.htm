What are the effect of Youth Unemployment?

01

If there are high chances of youth unemployment in the country, most of the students choose to visit another country for a job. Hence, it affects the workforce of the country.

02

Unemployment can also have a significant impact on a person's physical health. Unemployment is a very stressful situation, so it can cause stress-related health problems like heart disease, back pain, and insomnia.

03

For any country, development is possible only when the government is able to provide jobs for youths.

04

For most middle-class families, youth is the only way of income. If they are unemployed then there is no way of generating income for household expenditure.

05

Unemployed people have less family and marital satisfaction and increased family challenges.

06

High unemployment rates can cause civil unrest in a country.

07

When there is unemployment in society, people lose skills due to underutilization and this leads to loss of human capital.

08

Loss of income will result in loss of confidence, disappointment, negative attitude towards public affairs and a person's self-esteem will be hurt because of these mental illnesses.

09

Young people who can't find a job after trying so hard often wish to end their lives. As a result, more suicide cases are increasing year by year.
